About Chuck

My name is Chuck Wongus. I am a progressive political organizer. I have been working in social justice for almost two years now, and I absolutely love giving back to the world.

Living in a world were people are accepted for who they are is my dream in life. I have been working ardently to accept people and be open minded in life.

I grew up in Chester, VA. Chester is a small suburb, or as the residents like to call it "a village", outside of Richmond, VA.
